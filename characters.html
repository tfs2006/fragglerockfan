The Fraggles

Five friends who live in the caves and dance their cares away!

Gobo Fraggle

The Explorer & Leader

About Gobo

Gobo is the natural-born explorer and de facto leader of the Fraggle Five. Brave, adventurous, and endlessly curious, Gobo loves nothing more than exploring the uncharted territories of Fraggle Rock and reading the postcards his Uncle Traveling Matt sends from "Outer Space" (our world).

As the nephew of the legendary explorer Uncle Traveling Matt, Gobo feels a deep responsibility to follow in his uncle's footsteps. He's often the voice of reason among his friends, though his confidence can sometimes border on stubbornness.

  • Guitar player

Red Fraggle

The Athlete & Daredevil

About Red

Red is the most athletic and competitive Fraggle in the Rock. With boundless energy and a fierce determination to be the best at everything she does, Red excels at sports, swimming, and any physical challenge you can throw at her.

Though she can be impulsive and hot-headed, Red has a big heart and is fiercely loyal to her friends. Her best friend is Mokey, despite their opposite personalities, and she shares a competitive but affectionate relationship with Gobo.

Key Traits

  • Athletic champion
  • Competitive spirit
  • Energetic & impulsive
  • Confident
  • Can be impatient
  • Deeply caring

Mokey Fraggle

The Artist & Dreamer

About Mokey

Mokey is the gentle, artistic soul of the group. She finds beauty in everything around her and expresses herself through painting, poetry, and meditation. Mokey is deeply spiritual and has a special connection with Marjory the Trash Heap, whom she often visits for wisdom.

Despite her peaceful nature, Mokey is incredibly strong-willed and will stand up for her beliefs, especially when it comes to protecting others or preserving the beauty of Fraggle Rock. She's best friends with Red, and their opposite personalities create a perfect balance.

Key Traits

  • Artistic & creative

Wembley Fraggle

The Indecisive & Loyal Friend

About Wembley

Wembley is the sweetest and most lovable Fraggle you'll ever meet. His defining characteristic is his chronic indecisiveness—he can see both sides of every argument and struggles to make even the simplest choices. But what he lacks in decisiveness, he makes up for in loyalty and heart.

As Gobo's best friend and roommate, Wembley is always there to support his friends, even when he's terrified. He works as a Fireman for the Fraggle Rock Volunteer Fire Department, where his siren-playing skills come in handy.

Boober Fraggle

The Worrier & Chef

About Boober

Boober is the cautious, practical member of the Fraggle Five. While his friends are off having adventures, Boober prefers the safety and comfort of his cave, where he can indulge in his two favorite activities: cooking and doing laundry.

Though he's often pessimistic and worries about everything that could possibly go wrong, Boober has a heart of gold and genuinely cares about his friends' well-being. His cooking is legendary in Fraggle Rock, and his famous radish bars are a particular favorite.

The Doozers

Tiny, industrious builders who work all day, every day!

About the Doozers

The Doozers are a species of tiny, green, industrious creatures who stand only about 6 inches tall. They wear construction helmets and work clothes, and they love to build more than anything in the world.

Doozers spend their days constructing elaborate crystalline structures made from radishes. The amazing part? These beautiful constructions are meant to be eaten by the Fraggles! If the Fraggles didn't eat the Doozer structures, the Doozers would have nowhere left to build, which demonstrates the perfect ecosystem of Fraggle Rock.

Doozer Facts

  • Stand only 6 inches tall
  • Build structures from radishes
  • Work 24/7 and love it
  • Opposite of Fraggles in work ethic
  • Depend on Fraggles eating their work

The Gorgs

The "royal family" of giants who tend the garden

About the Gorgs

The Gorgs are a family of giant humanoid creatures who live in a castle in a garden adjacent to Fraggle Rock. They believe themselves to be the King and Queen of the Universe and their son to be the Crown Prince—though in reality, they may be the last of their species.

The Gorgs maintain an enormous garden where they grow radishes, which the Fraggles "steal" and the Doozers use for building. Junior Gorg spends much of his time trying (and failing) to catch the "Fwaggle" pests who raid the garden.

The Gorgs

Pa Gorg

King of the Universe

The self-proclaimed King who rules with bluster but has a kind heart beneath his gruff exterior. Deeply devoted to his wife and son.

Ma Gorg

Queen of the Universe

The practical and resourceful Queen who often has to manage both her husband and son. Loves her radish garden dearly.

Junior Gorg

Crown Prince

The gentle giant son who befriends the Fraggles (especially Red) and learns that kindness is more important than catching "pests."

Outer Space (Our World)

The mysterious realm beyond Fraggle Rock—also known as our world!

Doc

The Inventor

Doc is the human inventor who lives in the workshop connected to Fraggle Rock. He's kind, curious, and loves tinkering with inventions, completely unaware of the Fraggle civilization just beyond his wall.

Sprocket

Doc's Dog

Sprocket is Doc's faithful dog who often catches glimpses of Gobo when he comes through the hole to get Uncle Matt's postcards. Though Doc never believes him, Sprocket knows the Fraggles are real!

Uncle Traveling Matt

The Explorer

Gobo's uncle and the greatest Fraggle explorer, Uncle Matt ventured into Outer Space to explore the world of the "Silly Creatures" (humans). He sends postcards back to Gobo describing his often-hilarious misunderstandings of human culture.

Supporting Characters

Other beloved inhabitants of Fraggle Rock

Marjory the Trash Heap

The all-knowing oracle—a sentient compost heap who dispenses wisdom to the Fraggles. Attended by her heralds Philo and Gunge.

The Storyteller

An elderly Fraggle who shares ancient tales and legends of Fraggle Rock's history.

The World's Oldest Fraggle

The ancient keeper of Fraggle Rock's rules and traditions, though he's often asleep.

Convincing John

A charismatic Fraggle with the power to convince anyone of anything through his songs.
