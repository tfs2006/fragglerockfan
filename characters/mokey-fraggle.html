Mokey Fraggle

The Artist, Poet & Spiritual Soul of Fraggle Rock

Lavender-purple fur with mauve hair Performed by Kathryn Mullen Roommate: Red

Who is Mokey Fraggle?

Mokey Fraggle is the gentle, artistic soul of the Fraggle Five. A poet, painter, and philosopher, Mokey sees beauty in everything around her and expresses herself through various creative outlets. She represents the contemplative, spiritual side of Fraggle culture and often serves as the moral compass of the group.

As the roommate of the energetic Red Fraggle, Mokey demonstrates that opposites not only attract but can form the deepest friendships. While Red rushes through life, Mokey takes time to appreciate each moment. Their dynamic teaches viewers that different approaches to life are equally valid.

Mokey has a special connection with Marjory the Trash Heap, the all-knowing oracle of Fraggle Rock. She frequently visits Marjory for wisdom and guidance, and her spiritual nature makes her particularly receptive to the Trash Heap's sometimes cryptic advice.

Physical Appearance

Mokey's appearance reflects her gentle, dreamy nature. Her lavender-purple fur is soft and soothing to look at, and her long, flowing mauve hair often falls gracefully over her shoulders. She moves with an almost ethereal grace that matches her contemplative personality.

Physical Characteristics

  • Fur Color: Lavender-purple, soft and calming
  • Height: Approximately 18 inches (standard Fraggle size)
  • Hair: Long, flowing mauve hair
  • Movement: Graceful and unhurried
  • Expression: Usually serene, thoughtful, or dreamily content

Personality & Character Traits

Mokey's personality is defined by creativity, spirituality, and deep empathy:

Deeply Creative

Mokey expresses herself through painting, poetry, and various art forms. Creativity isn't just her hobby—it's how she processes the world.

Spiritually Connected

More in tune with nature and the spiritual realm than other Fraggles, Mokey seeks wisdom from Marjory and finds meaning in everyday moments.

Gentle & Empathetic

Mokey feels others' emotions deeply and rarely raises her voice. Her gentleness makes her an excellent mediator and confidant.

Strong Convictions

Despite her gentle nature, Mokey stands firm when she believes something is right. Her quiet strength surprises those who mistake gentleness for weakness.

The Dreamy Side

Mokey's contemplative nature sometimes means she's lost in thought when action is needed:

  • Dreaminess: Mokey can be so absorbed in her thoughts that she misses what's happening around her
  • Idealism: Her belief in the good of all creatures sometimes blinds her to real dangers
  • Sensitivity: She takes criticism hard and can be hurt by harsh words

Mokey's Artistic Pursuits

Art is central to Mokey's identity and her way of engaging with Fraggle Rock:

Poetry

Mokey writes poetry constantly, capturing the beauty she sees in everyday Fraggle life. Her poems range from whimsical to deeply philosophical.

Painting

Her cave is filled with paintings of Fraggle Rock's wonders. She particularly loves capturing the play of light on crystal formations.

Journaling

Mokey keeps detailed journals documenting life in Fraggle Rock, preserving its history through her observations.

Music

Like all Fraggles, Mokey loves music, but she brings a unique artistic sensibility to her performances.

Role in Fraggle Rock

The Radish Gatherer

Mokey has the dangerous job of harvesting radishes from the Gorgs' garden to feed the Fraggle community. This requires venturing into Gorg territory, making gentle Mokey surprisingly brave. Her connection to nature helps her navigate the garden and avoid Junior Gorg's attempts to catch "Fwaggles."

Spiritual Guide

When Fraggles need wisdom, Mokey often suggests visiting Marjory the Trash Heap. Her openness to spiritual guidance makes her the natural liaison between the Fraggle community and their oracle. She interprets Marjory's cryptic advice for her more practical-minded friends.

Emotional Support

Mokey is the friend everyone turns to when they need to talk. Her empathy and non-judgmental nature make her the perfect confidant, whether Boober is worried about germs or Wembley can't make a decision.

Key Relationships

Red Fraggle (Roommate & Best Friend)

Mokey and Red are the ultimate odd couple. Red's boundless energy contrasts with Mokey's calm nature, but they complement each other perfectly. Red pushes Mokey to action when needed, while Mokey helps Red slow down and appreciate life's quiet moments.

Marjory the Trash Heap (Spiritual Mentor)

Mokey has the closest relationship with Marjory of any Fraggle. She visits the oracle regularly, seeking wisdom and sharing her thoughts. This connection reflects Mokey's spiritual nature and openness to guidance.

Boober Fraggle (Kindred Spirit)

Though Boober is pessimistic and Mokey optimistic, they share a love of domestic arts. Boober cooks while Mokey creates, and both appreciate quiet, contemplative moments away from the chaos.

Cantus the Minstrel (Artistic Connection)

Mokey shares a special appreciation for Cantus and his philosophical approach to music. They both see deeper meaning in art and creativity.

Notable Mokey Episodes

Several episodes focus on Mokey's character development:

"Mokey and the Minstrels"

Mokey joins Cantus the Minstrel's traveling group, forcing her to choose between artistic growth and her friends. A beautiful exploration of following your dreams.

"The Trash Heap Doesn't Live Here Anymore"

When Marjory is threatened, Mokey leads the fight to save her spiritual mentor, showing that gentle souls can be fierce defenders.

"Mokey's Funeral"

When the others believe Mokey has died, the community must process grief—until Mokey returns, exploring themes of loss and appreciation.

"Red's Club"

Mokey is excluded from Red's exclusive club, testing their friendship and teaching lessons about inclusion and what really matters.

Famous Mokey Quotes

"There's beauty in everything if you look for it."

"Let's ask Marjory the Trash Heap!"

"The answers we seek are often within ourselves."

"Every creature has a song in their heart."

Behind the Scenes

Mokey Fraggle was performed by Kathryn Mullen, who brought ethereal grace and gentle warmth to the character. Mullen also performed Cotterpin Doozer, showcasing her range.

Production Notes

  • Performer: Kathryn Mullen (1983-1987)
  • Singing Voice: Kathryn Mullen
  • Design Inspiration: Created to embody creativity and spirituality
  • Name Origin: Has a soft, dreamy quality matching her personality
  • Also Performs: Cotterpin Doozer
