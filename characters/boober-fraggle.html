Boober Fraggle

The Cautious Worrier, Master Chef & Laundry Enthusiast

Blue-green fur with signature cap Performed by Dave Goelz Lives alone (prefers it)

Who is Boober Fraggle?

Boober Fraggle is the pessimistic, cautious, and surprisingly talented member of the Fraggle Five. While the other Fraggles rush off to play, Boober prefers the safety of his cave, the comfort of doing laundry, and the satisfaction of preparing a well-cooked meal. He sees danger everywhere—and he's occasionally right, which only reinforces his worrying.

But there's more to Boober than meets the eye. This fearful Fraggle is actually the best cook in all of Fraggle Rock, creating dishes that bring joy to everyone. He's also hiding a secret: an alter ego named Sidebottom who represents everything Boober suppresses—fun, spontaneity, and reckless abandon.

Boober's character explores important themes about anxiety, the value of caution, and how our fears can both protect and limit us. He teaches viewers that it's okay to be scared, and that quiet, careful people have their own kind of bravery.

Physical Appearance

Boober's appearance reflects his personality—muted, careful, and a bit disheveled. His blue-green (teal) fur is often covered by his signature oversized cap, which he pulls down over his eyes when stressed. He frequently looks worried, with wide eyes scanning for potential dangers.

Physical Characteristics

  • Fur Color: Blue-green (teal), somewhat muted
  • Height: Approximately 18 inches (standard Fraggle size)
  • Signature Item: Oversized knit cap that he pulls over his eyes
  • Hair: Reddish, usually hidden under his cap
  • Expression: Typically worried, suspicious, or concerned
  • Posture: Often hunched, protective

Personality & Character Traits

Boober's personality is a complex mix of fear, skill, and hidden depths:

Chronic Worrier

Boober sees potential disaster everywhere. Germs, cave-ins, bad luck—if it can go wrong, Boober has already imagined it going wrong.

Master Chef

Despite his pessimism, Boober is the most skilled cook in Fraggle Rock. His meals bring genuine joy to the community.

Laundry Lover

Boober's greatest passion is doing laundry. The methodical, clean, predictable nature of washing clothes brings him peace.

Secret Fun Side

Hidden within Boober is Sidebottom—an alter ego who loves fun, mischief, and everything Boober fears.

What Boober Fears

Boober's fears are extensive and specific:

  • Germs & Illness: Boober is convinced that sickness lurks everywhere
  • Bad Luck: He believes in superstitions and omens
  • Adventure: Any activity outside his routine terrifies him
  • The Unknown: New things could be dangerous things
  • Losing Control: He needs predictability and order

Sidebottom: Boober's Alter Ego

One of the most fascinating aspects of Boober's character is Sidebottom—his fun-loving, mischievous alter ego who emerges when Boober's repressed desires for fun become too strong to contain.

About Sidebottom

Sidebottom represents everything Boober suppresses:

  • Loves Fun: Sidebottom wants to play, dance, and celebrate
  • Reckless: He ignores all the dangers that obsess Boober
  • Mischievous: He enjoys pranks and causing harmless chaos
  • Fearless: Nothing scares Sidebottom—the opposite of Boober

The Sidebottom storylines explore the psychological concept that repressing parts of ourselves doesn't make them disappear—they just find other ways to emerge. Boober must learn to integrate some of Sidebottom's joy into his life without losing his careful nature entirely.

Boober's Skills & Contributions

Culinary Arts

Boober is, without question, the finest chef in Fraggle Rock. His cooking brings happiness to the entire community, showing that his careful, methodical nature has genuine value. Recipes that others would rush through, Boober perfects through patience and precision.

Laundry Excellence

While other Fraggles see laundry as a chore, Boober finds it meditative and fulfilling. His expertise in fabric care is unmatched, and he takes genuine pride in fresh, clean clothes. This running joke also represents how different people find joy in different activities.

Medical Knowledge

Boober's obsession with illness has given him extensive knowledge about Fraggle health and remedies. When someone is actually sick, Boober's expertise becomes genuinely valuable, and his worry transforms into competent care.

Key Relationships

Mokey Fraggle (Kindred Spirit)

Mokey and Boober share an appreciation for quiet, domestic activities. While she paints and writes poetry, he cooks and does laundry. They understand each other's need for calm and creativity, even if their temperaments differ.

Red Fraggle (Opposite Energy)

Red's fearless, athletic nature is everything Boober isn't. She often tries to drag him into activities, frustrated by his caution. Yet she's also protective of him when his fears threaten to overwhelm him.

Wembley Fraggle (Fellow Worrier)

Wembley and Boober both have anxious natures, creating a bond of understanding. They worry about different things—Wembley about disappointing others, Boober about danger—but they get each other's nervous energy.

Gobo Fraggle (Reluctant Adventures)

Gobo's adventures terrify Boober, but Gobo often needs Boober's skills on dangerous journeys. Their dynamic shows that every team needs both brave explorers and careful planners.

Role in Fraggle Rock

The Voice of Caution

When the Fraggle Five rushes into adventure, Boober points out what could go wrong. While often dismissed, he's sometimes right—and even when he's not, his caution prevents the group from being reckless.

Community Caretaker

Through cooking and care, Boober nurtures the Fraggle community in ways that matter. His contributions aren't flashy adventures, but they're essential to daily life in Fraggle Rock.

Comic Relief & Heart

Boober's exaggerated fears create humor, but his vulnerability also creates emotional depth. Episodes focusing on Boober often tackle serious themes about anxiety, identity, and self-acceptance.

Notable Boober Episodes

Several episodes focus on Boober's character development:

"Boober's Dream"

Boober's dreams reveal his deepest fears and desires, introducing viewers to the concept of Sidebottom and Boober's internal struggle.

"Boober Gorg"

Through magical mishap, Boober temporarily becomes a Gorg, forcing him to face his fears when he can no longer hide from danger.

"The Cavern of Lost Dreams"

Boober must venture into a dangerous cave, confronting his fears head-on to help his friends. Shows that courage is acting despite fear.

"Sidebottom Blues"

Sidebottom takes over completely, and Boober must reclaim himself while learning to integrate some of Sidebottom's joy into his life.

Famous Boober Quotes

"We're all going to die!"

"I knew this would happen. I just knew it."

"There's nothing more satisfying than a clean load of laundry."

"Fun is just another word for disaster waiting to happen."

Behind the Scenes

Boober Fraggle was performed by Dave Goelz, a legendary Muppet performer known for bringing complex, neurotic characters to life. Goelz also performed Gonzo the Great and would later perform Uncle Traveling Matt in Fraggle Rock.

Production Notes

  • Performer: Dave Goelz (1983-1987)
  • Singing Voice: Dave Goelz
  • Design Inspiration: Created to embody anxiety and hidden talents
  • Name Origin: Evokes "boo-boo" (mistake) and worry
  • Also Performs: Uncle Traveling Matt, Large Marvin

Dave Goelz brought incredible depth to Boober, making his fears feel real while keeping him lovable. Learn more on our Behind the Scenes page.

Why Boober Matters

Boober represents viewers who struggle with anxiety, fear, or feeling different from their more adventurous peers:

  • Validates Caution: Shows that being careful isn't the same as being weak
  • Hidden Talents: Demonstrates that quiet people have valuable skills
  • Anxiety Representation: One of TV's earliest honest portrayals of anxious personalities
  • Integration: Through Sidebottom, explores how we can accept all parts of ourselves

Boober's message is powerful: you don't have to be brave like Gobo or energetic like Red to matter. Your careful nature and quiet contributions are valuable too.
